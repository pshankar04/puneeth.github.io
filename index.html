Conversation Analysis: Social Networks over Surface and Dark Web.

The idea of this project was to contrast the difference in user conversations over 4 different social networking sites over a specific topic. Three of them - Twitter, Reddit, and 4chan from the surface web and Galaxy3 on the Dark web. We had compared the conversations (user posts and threads) made on a specific topic (technology, sports, etc) through a word and document embedding techniques (word2vec and doc2vec) and cluster the similar conversation threads based on k-means clustering technique. I had to train the model with an already existing doc2vec model to obtain efficient clustering. The final step was to name the cluster using the WordNet dictionary of synonyms.

Technologies Used:
Python3, pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, BeautifulSoup4, Gensim, Scikit-Learn.
